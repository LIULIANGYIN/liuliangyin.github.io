Rock Paper Scissors

ROCK PAPER SCISSORS是外國人在玩剪刀石頭布真正使用的字詞和正確順序，與台灣人常喊的PAPER SCISSORS STONE 有滿大的出入，希望透過圖形化海報的形式讓大家了解。

ROCK PAPER SCISSORS are the correct words and orders in foreigners, which are far different from "PAPER SCISSORS STONE" used by Taiwanese. Hope people can understand this through these graphic posters.
