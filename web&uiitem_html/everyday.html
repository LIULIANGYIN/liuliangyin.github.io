Everyday Diary App

Everday Personal Dairy App是一款日記App，現代人越來越少提起筆來寫字，要大家養成每天寫日記的習慣，不好維持。用手機能迅速記錄下當時的心情及發生的大小事。因此發展此套app，希望大家能把生活小細節記錄下來。
-
目標族群:平時有記錄生活習慣者、有寫日記習慣之使用者、總是無法持之以恆的使用者。目前為mockup階段。
-
設計流程：設計發想-資訊架構-流程規劃-線框初稿-視覺彩稿。以Sketch繪製彩稿。

People nowadays seldom write with pens and paper. So, it's hard to get into the habbit of keeping a diary. But you can rapidly note down your mood or things by phones! Hoping this app can let you remember the memories of life.
-
Target group:people who loves keeping a dairy, or those want to do it but can't.
-
Design process:
User Stories > Information Arctitecture > Logic Flow > Wireframe > Mockup, Mockup was drawn by Sketch.
