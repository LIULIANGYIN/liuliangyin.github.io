AiR Furniture Website

AiR Infatable Furniture為2017畢業展覽的主題，產品設計類。整體產品完成後，我們為AiR infatable Furniture建立品牌網站。網站方向以產品呈現為主，搭配視覺輔助圖形，營造充氣品牌的輕盈感受。
AiR為一款專為單身族群設計的充氣家具。從「單身經濟」的議題出發，並選用充氣材質做家具，希望不只為單身族群帶來便於收納、且具生活享受的設計!
團隊成員:譚雅云、陳凡、劉亮吟
個人負責：產品發想、設計實作、展場規劃、品牌網站規劃、前端開發

Enter Website

AiR infatable Furniture is the theme of our final team project, category of product design. After finishing our product, we decided to make a brand website of AiR. Main points are introducing and showing features of product. Creating graceful & light feelings of our inflatable brand.
An inflatable furniture designed for single groups. From the "single economy" issue, and the use of inflatable material to do furniture, hopes that not only bring convenience for the single groups, and also enjoy the design of life!
Responsible for design thinking, 1:1 solid modelling, exhibition planning, web design.
